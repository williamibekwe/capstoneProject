Accident Segmentation

Cluster 1 involves female drivers about 45 years old, driving weekday afternoons in clear weather.

Cluster 2 accidents involve a single-car and pedestrians in the evening in an urban setting. The driver is typically non-drunk.

Cluster 3 accidents occur during the morning commute with non-drunk drivers and clear weather on large highways.

Cluster 4 involves drunk drivers about 36 years old driving late at night in severe weather.

Cluster 5 drivers are older males (60+ years old) driving in clear weather in the afternoon.

Cluster 6 involves young, non-drunk, male drivers (~29 years old) in the afternoon.

Cluster 7 involves young, drunk, male drivers driving late at night on the weekend in clear weather.

Cluster 8 involves older (around 48 years old), non-drunk drivers battling severe weather in the afternoon.
